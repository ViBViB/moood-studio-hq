## 1. Visual Strategy Diagnosis
Anthros is a high-end ergonomic brand. The design language is **Technical, Authoritative, and Clinical but Premium**. It uses a strong combination of anatomical illustration, high-fidelity 3D renders, and a very structured typographic hierarchy.

- **Primary Font:** Clarika Pro Grot (Custom Loaded)
- **Primary Palette:** Cyan (#00B5E2) on Deep Black and Beige (#F4F1EA).
- **Style Rules:**
    - Titles are often all-caps and use Heavy/Black weights.
    - Large whitespace between technical sections.
    - Information is displayed in "Scientific Data" formats (grids, metrics, diagrams).
