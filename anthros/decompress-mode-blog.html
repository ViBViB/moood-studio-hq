Experience Ultimate Freedom with the Anthros Decompress Mode

Redefine the way you sit

At Anthros, we believe that the way you sit affects the way you feel. That’s why every feature we design is built to help you move better, feel better, and live better, starting with your chair.


Decompress Mode redefines what comfort and control look like in an ergonomic office chair. Whether you’re deep in focus or ready to decompress, this new system gives you the freedom to sit exactly how your body needs.

Unrestricted Control

Five Tilt Positions for a Healthier, More Productive You

With the Anthros three-position tilt, you’re in control. Choose from three distinct locked positions, and two unlocked—each engineered for a different type of work, focus, or recovery.

Movement is medicine. When you sitting matches your task, your performance peaks.

Locked Positions

1

Active Mode: Forward Tilt
(-2° Tilt)

Perfect for active work sessions and deep focus. This position keeps your pelvis slightly forward, promoting a natural spinal curve and encouraging core engagement.

2

Standard mode (1° Mid Position)

Your all-day comfort zone. This balanced setting allows subtle movement while maintaining healthy upright alignment, ideal for long stretches of focused work.

3

Decompress Mode (5° Tilt)

The perfect balance between alert and at ease. Relieves pressure off your spine and hips while keeping you engaged enough to stay productive.

Unlocked Positions

1

Free Float Decompress Mode

Allows subtle movement and micro-adjustments as you work. Use the tension control to "float" between positions, promoting circulation and preventing stiffness.

2

Full Decompress Mode

Ideal for a brief break or stretch. Lean back to gently lengthen the spine and unload pressure from the lower back, offering a moment of true gravitational relief.

The Core Principle

Why It Matters: The Science of Healthy Sitting

Sitting shouldn’t mean staying still. Traditional office chairs lock your body into one static position, leading to pain, fatigue, and long-term discomfort.

The Anthros Decompress Mode encourages micro-movement and adaptability, which helps reduce spinal compression, improves circulation, and prevents muscle fatigue.

When your chair moves with you, your body stays engaged and your mind stays clear.

Constant Innovation

The Evolution of Comfort

At Anthros, innovation never stops. Each update is built on our deep understanding of biomechanics and real customer feedback.

The new three position tilt is another step forward—a symbol of how we’re continuing to evolve to give you the most personalized comfort possible.
