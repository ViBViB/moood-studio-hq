The Vision

The Vision

The buildings sector accounts for nearly 40% of global carbon emissions. [CLIENT_NAME] exists to change that — by making whole-life carbon assessment fast, accessible, and actionable for the architects, developers, and planning authorities who shape the built environment.

That's a mission worth leading. But right now, the world can't see it clearly.

The goal of this project is to close that gap. A brand system that commands authority in a market moving toward mandatory compliance. A website that converts the visitors who are already arriving. An identity that makes [CLIENT_NAME] look exactly as serious as the problem it's solving.

This is a two-phase, fully integrated project — Brand Identity first, Website second. The brand becomes the foundation. Everything built on top of it is consistent, intentional, and built to last.

The Problem

The Diagnosis

Visitors arrive. They don't convert. The traffic is there — the site isn't closing it. The gap between arrival and action is a narrative problem: the value isn't being communicated in a way that maps to how users actually think about their workflow.
Science-driven language is losing the room. [CLIENT_NAME]'s current messaging leads with technical rigour — which builds credibility but doesn't build urgency. The shift from "science-driven" to "user-value" isn't a dumbing down. It's the difference between describing the engine and selling the drive.
The platform is blocking the brand. HubSpot Starter wasn't built for premium design. Every update is a workaround, and the design ceiling is too low for where [CLIENT_NAME] needs to go. The right platform removes the friction between your vision and what users actually see.
