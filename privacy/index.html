Privacy Policy

Who we are

Moood.Studio is a design and engineering studio. We build brand identities, websites, and design systems for ambitious companies. Our website is moood.studio.

What we collect

When you engage with us through a proposal or intake form, we collect the information you provide directly — your name, email address, and project details. We use this solely to communicate with you about your project.

We do not collect analytics data, track browsing behavior, or use third-party advertising cookies on our client-facing pages.

How we use your information

Your contact details are used exclusively to respond to your inquiry, send you proposals, and manage the project engagement. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Data storage

Project data is stored securely using Upstash Redis. Emails are delivered via Resend. Neither service uses your data for advertising purposes.

Your rights
