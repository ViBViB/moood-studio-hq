# Brand Identity Quote Form Content

## Section 1: Contact Information & Privacy
Welcome! Please fill out the details below so we can get to know you and your project.

*   Company Name
*   Full Name
*   Role / Job Title (e.g., Founder, Marketing Manager)
*   Email Address
*   Current Website / Social Media (If applicable)

**Privacy Note:** Your privacy is important to us. The information provided in this form will be used solely for the purpose of preparing your custom quote and will never be shared with third parties.

## Section 2: Business Profile (The "Who")
Help us understand the soul of your business.

*   **Brand Name & Tagline:** (Exactly as they should appear in the logo).
*   **Business Description:** Briefly describe your company and the primary services or products you offer.
*   **Target Audience:** Who is your ideal customer? (Age, interests, and location).
*   **Brand Values:** List 3-5 keywords that define your brand (e.g., Innovative, Trustworthy, Bold, Elegant).

## Section 3: Identity Diagnosis (The "Why")
Identifying the goals of this visual shift.

*   **Project Status:** Is this a new brand launch or a redesign of an existing identity?
*   **If a redesign:** What is currently not working with your existing brand, and why are you choosing to change it now?
*   **Competitive Advantage:** What makes your brand different or better than your direct competitors?

## Section 4: Creative Scope & Deliverables (The "What")
Defining the technical requirements for the logo, palette, and typography.

*   **Primary Applications:** Where will the brand be used most? (e.g., Social Media, Website, Physical Packaging, Signage).
*   **Color Preferences:** Are there any specific colors you love, or any "forbidden" colors we should avoid?
*   **Typography Style:** Do you prefer Serif (traditional), Sans Serif (modern), Script (handwritten), or Display (bold/unique) fonts?
*   **Visual Direction:** On a scale of 1 to 5, where does your brand sit?
    *   Minimalist [ 1 — 2 — 3 — 4 — 5 ] Complex
    *   Traditional [ 1 — 2 — 3 — 4 — 5 ] Modern
    *   Playful [ 1 — 2 — 3 — 4 — 5 ] Serious

## Section 5: Logistics & Expectations (The "When")
Setting the stage for the partnership.

*   **Visual Inspiration:** Please provide links (Pinterest/Behance) or upload images of styles that resonate with you.
*   **Estimated Launch Date:** When do you need the final source files delivered?
*   **Investment Range:** Please select your estimated budget (e.g., $1k–$3k, $3k–$5k, $5k+).
*   **Additional Comments:** Is there anything else we should know?
